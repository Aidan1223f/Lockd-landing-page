## 4. How We Protect Your Information

### 4.1 Security Measures

**Encryption:**
- All data is encrypted in transit (TLS/SSL)
- Sensitive data is encrypted at rest
- Passwords are hashed with bcrypt

**Payment Security:**
- We use Stripe (PCI DSS Level 1 certified)
- We never store full credit card numbers
- We never store CVV codes
- Card data goes directly to Stripe's secure servers

**Identity Protection:**
- We only collect SSN last 4 digits (not full SSN)
- Identity data is encrypted
- Access is restricted to authorized personnel only

**Access Controls:**
- Multi-factor authentication for our team
- Regular security audits
- Limited employee access to sensitive data
- Activity logging and monitoring

### 4.2 Data Retention

**Account Data:**
- Kept as long as your account is active
- Deleted within 90 days of account closure

**Transaction Data:**
- Kept for 7 years (required for tax purposes)
- Personal identifiers removed after 90 days

**Identity Verification:**
- Kept for 5 years (required by law)
- Securely deleted afterward

**Marketing Data:**
- Deleted immediately upon opt-out

### 4.3 Limitations

No system is 100% secure. While we use industry-standard security:
- We can't guarantee absolute security
- You're responsible for keeping your password safe
- Report suspicious activity immediately
